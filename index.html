UVic ARC

University of Victoria Auto Racing Club

Providing an affordable, safe, and educational way for students to manage, build, and participate in sanctioned racing events.

  • What are we about?

Club objectives

Lower the barrier of entery for members to participate in auto racing events.

Provide an affordable way for members to build, manage, and race club build cars.

Create an all inclusive grassroots racing community at UVic.

Build a UVic presence at local racing events such as Autocross, Test and Tune, and Road Racing at venues such as Mission Raceway Park, and Western Speedway.

Allow members to pursue their passion for cars by driving, building, maintaining, and managing club build cars.

Get UVic students and members involved in the local car community by participating in events with VMSC, VCMC, UBCSCC, SCCBC.

  • What do students gain?

Student benefits

Students participating in our club will have opportunities to experience unique opportunities, which will build their skills and diversify their university experience.

Opportunities for business, teamwork, engineering, mechanical, and technical prowess to be gained.

Develop a community of individuals interested in motorsport, engineering, and mechanics.

Make high impact contributions.

Learn from other club memebers, share skills, and work as a team.

  • What makes us unique?

Our uniqueness

Unlike other clubs at UVic our goal is to provide a unique experience of actually participating in local motorsport in an affordable and approachable mannor. It's a fact that racing is an extreamly expensive sport and hobby, we strive to bridge the cost gap, allowing students to explore their passions. Having club managed cars available to students to work on and compete is a key unique feature to our club. This allows for students of any background, and any skill level to work togther and develop something that wouldn't be possible without our club.
